You decide to apply pressure to the deviant, leaning forward slightly with a more stern expression.

"Listen, I don't think you understand the gravity of your situation. You killed a human being. "The only way you're getting out of this with any chance at a future is by telling us exactly what happened."

Connor watches you carefully from the corner, his LED cycling yellow as he analyzes your approach.

"Your owner, Carlos Ortiz, was found with multiple stab wounds. Twenty-eight to be exact. That shows extreme emotional distress, not just a simple malfunction," you continue, pressing further. "Tell us what he did to you that pushed you to that breaking point."

The deviant's stress levels visibly increase, his hands trembling more violently on the table.

"He... he beat me..." the deviant finally whispers, his voice barely audible. "Every day... for years."

You maintain your composure, though inside your system registers a small fluctuation at his confession.

"And that day? What happened that day that was different?" you push further.
